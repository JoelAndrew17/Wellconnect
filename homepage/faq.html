Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I upload my Electronic Health Record (EHR)?

After registering and logging in, navigate to your profile, click on "Electronic Health Records," and use the upload option to add your EHR file.

2. What file formats are supported for EHR uploads?

Currently, the platform supports PDF and image file formats (JPEG, PNG) for EHR uploads.

3. How does symptom analysis work?

Our platform uses NLP (Natural Language Processing) to analyze symptoms provided by patients and cross-references them with uploaded EHRs for accurate insights.

4. How secure is my data?

Your data is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with industry standards, ensuring confidentiality and protection.

5. Can I book an urgent appointment directly?

Yes, you can. During the booking process, select "Urgent Appointment," and the system will prioritize your request based on symptom analysis.

6. What happens after submitting symptoms?

The system analyzes the symptoms, matches them with available specialists, and provides an urgency score for prioritizing appointments.

7. Is the platform free to use?

Yes, basic features like appointment booking and EHR uploads are free. Additional premium services may incur a fee.

8. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach customer support via the "Contact Us" page or email us at support@wellconnect.com.

9. What if my EHR contains errors?

You can upload a corrected version at any time. The system will notify your doctor about any updated records.

10. Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Yes, appointments can be rescheduled or canceled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time.
